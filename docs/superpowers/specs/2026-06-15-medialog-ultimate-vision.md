# MediaLog Ultimate — The Vision

**Date:** 2026-06-15
**Status:** Strategic Vision (Draft)

## The Goal
To build the "Singular Bucket": a tool that doesn't just store links, but consumes, organizes, and helps you master information. It aims to supersede the fragmented workflow of **Readwise (Ingestion/Review)**, **Instapaper/Reader (Consumption)**, and **Obsidian/Notion (Synthesis)**.

## Core Philosophy
1.  **Source ≠ System:** Inboxes (tabs, feeds, newsletters) are noise. MediaLog is the signal.
2.  **Triage is Mandatory:** Information is only useful if it's placed in a context (Topic).
3.  **Retention is the Product:** If you don't remember it, you didn't learn it.
4.  **Synthesis is Automated:** The AI agent synthesizes your disparate notes into cohesive topic documents.

---

## Pillar 1: The High-Fidelity Ingestion Engine
*Readwise's core value is getting data IN effortlessly. MediaLog Ultimate expands this to multi-media.*

- **Full-Text Mirroring:** When a URL is added, MediaLog doesn't just fetch a title; it scrapes the full text and images using a "Readability" engine. This text is stored in the DB, making your library **permanently offline-accessible and searchable** even if the source goes down.
- **YouTube Transcript Sync:** For video links, automatically pull the transcript. Allow "Timestamp Highlighting" — clicking a highlight jumps the video to that moment.
- **Physical to Digital (OCR):** Mobile-first capture of physical book pages or handwritten notes via OCR, instantly assigned to a topic.
- **Newsletter Terminal:** A private `@medialog.io` email address that turns newsletters into "Active" entries in your Inbox, not your cluttered email app.

## Pillar 2: The "Live-in" Reader
*Why leave the app to read? MediaLog should be the best place to consume.*

- **Distraction-Free Mode:** A built-in reader view for every article/PDF/Transcript.
- **Inline Highlighting:** Selecting text inside the reader creates a "Highlight Entry" linked to the parent source. No more copy-pasting into notes.
- **Ghostreader AI:**
    - **Summarize:** Get the gist in 3 bullets before reading.
    - **Contextualize:** "How does this article relate to my other entries in the #AI topic?"
    - **Un-block:** Highlight a complex term/concept for an instant AI explanation.

## Pillar 3: Mastery through Spaced Repetition (SRS)
*Revisit 1.0 was a queue. Revisit 2.0 is a memory machine.*

- **True SRS Algorithm:** Replace "Least Recently Seen" with an SM2-style algorithm (Anki). High-quality highlights appear more frequently until they are mastered.
- **AI-Generated Active Recall:** The AI turns your highlights into "Cloze Deletion" or "Question/Answer" pairs.
- **The Daily Streak:** A low-friction daily review (3-5 minutes) that guarantees you never forget the "Done" material.

## Pillar 4: The Living Synthesis (Obsidian Killer)
*Obsidian's mess comes from the burden of manual linking. MediaLog automates the connections.*

- **Living Topic Documents:** Every topic has a "Master Note" that is **auto-generated by AI**. It synthesizes all entries, highlights, and your personal notes into a structured Wikipedia-style page for that topic.
- **Bi-directional Discovery:** When viewing an entry, the sidebar shows "Related Entries" across *other* topics based on semantic similarity.
- **Knowledge Export:** While the app is a cloud-native PWA, it maintains the "Plain Text First" promise. The Markdown export includes the AI-generated syntheses and all high-fidelity data.
