5. Brainstorm solutions: "What are some potential solutions to address [pain point] for [niche group]?"
6. Consider monetization: "How could a solution for [pain point] be monetized? What business models might work?"
7. Evaluate feasibility: "What resources or skills would be needed to implement [potential solution]?"
