## Monday: Lower Body Strength & Plyometrics
## Warm-up

Dynamic stretching (10 minutes)
Jump rope (3 minutes)

### Main Exercises

Back Squats

4 sets of 5 reps at 80% 1RM
Rest: 2-3 minutes


Romanian Deadlifts

3 sets of 8 reps
Rest: 90 seconds


Box Jumps

5 sets of 5 reps
Rest: 60 seconds


Depth Jumps

4 sets of 6 reps
Rest: 90 seconds



### Accessory Exercises

Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
Kettlebell Swings: 3 sets of 12 reps
Squat Jumps: 3 sets of 10 reps

Cool-down

Static stretching (10 minutes)

---

## Tuesday: Basketball Skills & Conditioning
### Warm-up

Light jogging and dynamic stretching (10 minutes)

Basketball Drills (60 minutes)

Ball handling series (15 minutes)
Shooting drills (20 minutes)

Spot-up shooting
Pull-up jumpers
Free throws


Defensive slides and closeouts (10 minutes)
Pick and roll scenarios (15 minutes)

### Conditioning

Full-court sprints: 10 sets of baseline to baseline
Rest: 30 seconds between sprints

Cool-down

Light shooting and stretching (10 minutes)

---

## Wednesday: Upper Body & Core
### Warm-up

Arm circles and band work (10 minutes)

Main Exercises

Bench Press

4 sets of 6 reps at 75% 1RM
Rest: 90 seconds


Pull-ups or Lat Pulldowns

4 sets of 8 reps
Rest: 60 seconds


Push Press

3 sets of 6 reps
Rest: 90 seconds


Medicine Ball Slams

4 sets of 10 reps
Rest: 60 seconds



Core Work

Planks: 3 sets of 45 seconds
Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps

Cool-down

Static stretching (10 minutes)

---

## Thursday: Speed & Agility
### Warm-up

Dynamic stretching and light jogging (10 minutes)

Speed & Agility Drills

Ladder Drills (3 sets of each)

In-and-out
Lateral shuffle
High knees


Cone Drills (3 sets of each)

5-10-5 drill
T-drill
X-drill


Resisted Sprints

5 sets of 20-meter sprints
Rest: 60 seconds between sprints


Plyometric Circuit (3 rounds)

Hurdle hops: 10 reps
Bounding: 20 meters
Single-leg hops: 10 each leg



Basketball-Specific Conditioning

Full Court back and forth shots 5 spots

Cool-down

Light jogging and stretching (10 minutes)

---

## Friday: Full Body Power & Basketball Skills
### Warm-up

Dynamic stretching and jump rope (10 minutes)

Power Exercises

Power Cleans

5 sets of 3 reps
Rest: 2 minutes


Jump Squats

4 sets of 6 reps
Rest: 90 seconds


Plyometric Push-ups

3 sets of 8 reps
Rest: 60 seconds


Single-arm Dumbbell Snatches

3 sets of 5 reps each arm
Rest: 60 seconds



### Basketball Skills (45 minutes)

Spot-up shooting from game situations (15 minutes)
Dribble moves to shot series (15 minutes)
Free throw practice (15 minutes)

Conditioning

Quarter court 1-on-1 games: Play to 5, winner stays on (15 minutes)

Cool-down

Static stretching and foam rolling (15 minutes)


Saturday: Game Day / Open Run

Participate in competitive basketball games or scrimmages
Focus on applying skills and explosive movements in game situations
Duration: 1-2 hours


Sunday: Active Recovery

Light jogging or cycling (20-30 minutes)
Yoga or extended stretching session (30 minutes)
Foam rolling and self-massage
Review game film or basketball strategy


Notes:

Adjust weights and intensities based on your current fitness level.
Always prioritize proper form over weight or speed to prevent injuries.
Stay well-hydrated and maintain a balanced diet rich in protein and complex carbohydrates.
Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours) for optimal recovery and performance.
Listen to your body and take additional rest if needed.

Periodic Assessments (Every 4-6 Weeks):

Vertical Jump Test
Sprint Time (20m and 40m)
Agility T-Test
1RM for main lifts (Squat, Bench Press, Power Clean)
