"A source is not
a system."

Your browser tabs are not a reading list. Your "Save for later" folder is a graveyard. Bookmarks are where links go to die.

The problem isn't capture — it's what happens after. Nothing gets revisited. Nothing gets synthesized. Nothing gets remembered.

MediaLog is the system, not another inbox. Every link is placed in a topic, triaged, consumed, and revisited on a schedule that builds retention.
